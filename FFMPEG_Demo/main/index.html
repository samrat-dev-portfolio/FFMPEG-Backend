Tales Of Bhola Grandpa

BIOGRAPHICAL SKETCH OF THE AUTHOR

Manoj Das is an author who writes in Oriya and English, is one of the foremost short story writers of India. Manoj Das’s fictionary characters are from varied backgrounds and display different dimensions of human nature. As of date, he has written more than eighty books, comprising of poems, short stories, novels and articles on history and culture.

He has been bestowed with many prestigious awards like Sahitya Akademi Award(1972), Padma Shri(2001) and many others for his excellence in writing.

In the story-’Tales of Bhola Grandpa’ he uses the simplicity and forgetful nature of the protagonist to make the story a hilarious one.
